TASKLIST PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE GENERAL PURPOSE STEAM TURBINE
PM-W2 (Every 2 Weeks)
No. Maintenance Tasklist
1. Toolbox meeting and preparation (tools, man power, etc)
2. Check governor lube oil level. Make up if necessary
3. Check bearing housing lube oil level (for ring oiled system).
4. Check oil reservoir lube oil level (for pressurized oil system).
5. Check bearing housing and lubrication temperature
6. Check turbine speed (by installed tachometer or portable tachometer)
7. Check smoothness of operation (abnormal noises or sudden operatio change)
8. Good house keeping and finishing
PM-M3 (Every 3 Months)
No. Maintenance Tasklist
1. Toolbox meeting and preparation (tools, man power, etc)
2. Check governor lube oil level. Make up if necessary
3. Replace bearing housing lube oil (for ring oiled system). Visual check the drain lube oil from deposit, sludge, or contaminant
4. Drain small quantity of lube oil from lube oil reservoir (for pressurized oil system). Perform lube oil analysis in PERTAMINA Laboratory
5. Good house keeping and finishing
PM-M6 (Every 6 Months)
No. Maintenance Tasklist
1. Toolbox meeting and preparation (tools, man power, etc)
2. Remove steam turbine coupling
3. Inspect steam turbine coupling & cleaning. Replace if necessary as per recommendation Steam
4. Turbine Inspection:
- Check steam turbine endplay or axial movement. Adjust if necessary.
- Check radial lift rotor
- Inspect tachometer condition
5. Cleaning water jacket (if any)
6. Replace governor lube oil.
7. Replace bearing housing lube oil (for ring oiled system). Visual check the drain lube oil from deposit, sludge, or contaminant
8. Drain small quantity of lube oil from lube oil reservoir (for pressurized oil system). Perform lube oil analysis in PERTAMINA Laboratory
9. Steam Turbine Exercise:
- Exercise and lubricate sentinel valve. (Please coordinated with operation team to exercise the sentinel valve)
- Close exhaust valve slowly. Always check steam exhaust pressure.
- When sentinel valve takes action, check the exhaust pressure. Exercise safety trip shutoff device or trip valve, either by overspeed trip or unlatch the trip lever (if possible). Check the turbine speed after being tripped.
- Exercise governor and throttle valve assembly by throttling steam supply to the turbine.
- Check governor linkage and governor valve movement or action during exercise. Governor shall be move smoothly.
- Replace any worn parts
10. Alignment check. Adjust if necessary
11. Install steam turbine coupling
12. Good house keeping and finishing
PM-M12 (Every 1 Year or 12 Months)
No. Maintenance Tasklist
1. Toolbox meeting and preparation (tools, man power, etc)
2. Remove steam turbine coupling lnspect steam turbine coupling & cleaning. Replace if necessary as per recommendation Steam
4. Turbine Inspection:
- Check steam turbine endplay or axial movement. Adjust if necessary
- Check radial lift rotor
- Remove upper casing turbine (Horizontal Split). Remove governor, hub coupling, bearing housing, and gland housing (Vertical Split)
- Cleaning steam turbine casing
- Inspect journal bearing (visual and clearance measurement. Replace if necessasry as per recommendation
- Inspect locating or ball bearing. Replace if necessary as per recommendation
- Replace carbon packing and inspect carbon packing ex-use (condition and clearance)
- Inspect rotor condition (shaft bearing bearing area, carbon packing area, wheel, bucket, etc) (visual and critical dimension)
- Inspect labyrinth seal or seal ring (visual, clearance). Replace if necessary as per recommendation
- Inspect governor coupling or gear
- Inspect tachometer coupling or gear
- Install upper casing turbine (Horizontal Split). Install governor, hub coulling, bearing housing, and gland housing (Vertical Split)
- Inspect tachometer condition
5. Cleaning water jacket (if any)
6. Replace governor lube oil. Replace bearing housing lube oil (for ring oiled system). Visual check the drain lube oil from deposit, sludge, or contaminant Drain small quantity of lube oil from lube oil reservoir (for pressurized oil system). Perform lube oil analysis in PERTAMINA Laboratory.
9. Steam Turbine Exercise:
- Exercise and lubricate sentinel valve. (Please coordinated with operation team to exercise the sentinel valve)
- Close exhaust valve slowly. Always check steam exhaust pressure.
- When sentinel valve takes action, check the exhaust pressure. Exercise safety trip shutoff device or trip valve, either by overspeed trip or unlatch the trip lever (if possible). Check the turbine speed after being tripped.
- Exercise governor and throttle valve assembly by throttling steam supply to the turbine.
- Check governor linkage and governor valve movement or action during exercise. Governor shall be move smoothly.
- Replace any worn parts
10. Alignment check. Adjust if necessary
11. Install steam turbine coupling
12. Good house keeping and finishing
